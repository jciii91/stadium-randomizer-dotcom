Pokémon Stadium Randomizer

How to Use:

Use the file select button below to choose your copy of Pokémon Stadium for modification. The website only supports US v1.0 (checksum 72F66F05) at this time. Adjust the sliders to set the level of randomization you want, then proceed with patching your game. In addition to any randomization options you select, a built-in Rental Randomizer has been added. Press START when choosing or registering a team for the Gym Leader Castle to activate. PLEASE NOTE: Expansion pack memory must be enabled.

Normal

Controls the level of randomization applied to Pokémon base stats.

Normal

Applies new stats and assigns new moves to the rental Pokémon available in Round 1 of Gym Castle.

Normal

Changes the Pokémon used by trainers in Gym Castle Round 1.
